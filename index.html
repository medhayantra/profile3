About US

Medhayantra is a leading private company based in Nepal, specializing in advanced IoT-based sensors and automation solutions. Our expertise spans across various domains, including smart agriculture, precision farming, industrial automation, cold store monitoring, and home automation. With a focus on precision agriculture, we provide cutting-edge solutions that enhance farm productivity and sustainability through real-time monitoring and automated systems. Our offerings in IoT automation and sensor technologies enable businesses to streamline operations, improve efficiency, and reduce costs across multiple sectors. Medhayantra has collaborated with prestigious research institutions such as the National Agriculture Research Center (NARC), National Citrus Research Program (NCRP), and Regional Agriculture Research Center (RARS), gaining invaluable insights into the latest advancements in agriculture. This enables us to continually refine and enhance our products and services, ensuring they meet the highest standards of innovation and reliability. Our diverse range of services includes: Smart Home & Commercial Building Solutions Real-Time Farm Monitoring Cold Store Monitoring Systems Smart Manufacturing Metrics & KPIs Monitoring Tools Industrial Design & Automation IoT Sensors for Smart Measurement & Data Logging Groundwater Level Indicators Rainfall Simulators Soil Moisture & Temperature Monitoring Smart Water Management Systems At Medhayantra, our mission is to deliver innovative and tailored IoT-based sensor and automation solutions that help optimize operations, minimize waste, and improve efficiency. We are committed to becoming a trusted leader in providing advanced IoT solutions not only in Nepal but also in global markets.
